About Us

SEDS (Students for the Exploration and Development of Space) is an international organization aiming to promote space exploration and development. We do this by involving the educational sector through many school and engineering projects. We, SEDS Nebula are one of its chapters at VIT Bhopal University. Our aim is to create awareness about space exploration activities by involving students and making them realize where their true passion lies by means of projects, outreach, space talks, space events, competitions, visits and other engaging activities. Our aim is not just to raise a new generation of inquisitive learners but also the innovative shapers of the future society who will in turn inspire others to follow and learn more about their dreams.
